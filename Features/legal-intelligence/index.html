Legal Intelligence

AI-Powered Legal Research & Analysis

Find relevant cases, statutes, and precedents in seconds. Analyze legal arguments and develop winning strategies with AI that understands legal reasoning.

What is Legal Intelligence?

Legal Intelligence is an AI-powered research assistant that helps lawyers find relevant cases, statutes, and legal information quickly. It understands legal concepts and reasoning, not just keywords, so you get exactly what you need.

Think of it as having a brilliant legal researcher working around the clock - one who reads millions of cases instantly and helps you build stronger arguments based on the law.

How Does It Help Lawyers?

  • Finds relevant cases and statutes in seconds instead of hours of manual searching
  • Analyzes how similar cases were decided across different jurisdictions
  • Identifies strengths and weaknesses in legal arguments
  • Suggests counterarguments and responses to opposing counsel's positions
  • Creates properly formatted citations and legal summaries automatically

Common Uses

Case Research

Find relevant precedents and similar cases that support your legal position.

Legal Brief Writing

Get suggestions for stronger arguments and supporting case law as you draft.

Regulatory Compliance

Stay updated on changing laws and regulations affecting your clients.

Case Strategy

Analyze potential outcomes based on similar cases and judicial tendencies.

How It Works - Simple Steps

  1. Ask Your Legal Question
    Type your question in plain English, just as you would ask a colleague
  2. Review Instant Results
    See relevant cases, statutes, and legal analyses with key passages highlighted
  3. Refine Your Search
    Ask follow-up questions or request specific information about cases found
