What is Batch Review?

Batch Review is a tool that helps lawyers review many legal documents at once. Instead of reading each document one by one, you can upload multiple documents and our system will analyze them all together.

This is especially helpful for tasks like due diligence (checking contracts before a business deal), discovery (reviewing documents for a lawsuit), or compliance reviews (making sure documents follow legal rules).

How Does It Help Lawyers?

  • Saves Time: Review 50+ documents in hours instead of days or weeks
  • Finds Important Information: Automatically highlights key clauses like termination rights, payment terms, and liability limits
  • Spots Problems: Identifies missing clauses, unusual terms, or potential legal risks
  • Creates Reports: Generates simple summaries of what was found across all documents

Common Uses

Contract Review

Quickly analyze vendor agreements, leases, or employment contracts to find unusual terms or missing protections.

Due Diligence

Review all contracts and agreements before a merger or acquisition to identify potential risks or liabilities.

Litigation Discovery

Sort through large document collections to find relevant evidence and key information for your case.

Compliance Checks

Verify that all your documents comply with new regulations or legal requirements.
